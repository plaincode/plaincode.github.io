iContacts+

What was the name of the person I met yesterday?

This app helps you finding your contacts in your address book!

Features

  • Order contacts by Creation/Editing Date
  • Find contacts by parts of contact details and/or number
  • Call/View/Edit contacts inside application
  • Complete privacy: no contact data is transferred anywhere!

… More to come to help you organising your contacts soon!

Latest update includes

  • Corrected display of last name
  • Sort by first or last name can be configured
  • Enhanced Result View
  • More Translations
  • Updated for iOS 14+ with modern Contact framework
