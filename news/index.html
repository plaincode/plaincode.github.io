The One That Got Away: 3DTouch Scale

January 1, 2026

Happy New Year! For our first post of 2026, we're sharing a fun piece of plaincode history. Back in the iPhone 6s era, we developed an innovative app called 3DTouch Scale that turned your iPhone into a digital weight scale using the pressure-sensitive 3D Touch sensors.

The concept was brilliant (if we say so ourselves): measure fruits directly on the screen, or use a clever finger-balancing technique for non-capacitive objects. We even made a demo video! Clinometer App Releases Version 3.0 for Android: Enhanced Compatibility and Stability

October 28, 2024

We are excited to announce the release of Clinometer App Version 3.0 for Android devices. This major update brings full compatibility with the latest Android operating systems and introduces crucial stability fixes to enhance user experience.

Clinometer has long been a trusted tool for professionals and hobbyists alike, providing precise angle and slope measurements typically within ¬±0.1 degrees. Whether aligning a picture frame or undertaking complex engineering tasks, users rely on Clinometer for its accuracy and ease of use.

Key Features of Version 3.0:

  • Full Compatibility with Newer Android Versions: Ensures seamless performance across all modern Android devices.
  • Stability Enhancements: Addresses previous issues to provide a smoother and more reliable user experience.

Clinometer Version 3.0 is now available for download on the Google Play Store. Existing users can update the app to access the new features and improvements, and all previous in-app purchases will continue to be supported.
